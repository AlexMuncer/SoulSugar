Soul Sugar – Sweet Sounds for Every Occasion

From dancefloor disco to party anthems, Soul Sugar bring the perfect soundtrack to your event. Frequently Asked Questions

HOW DO I BOOK SOUL SUGAR? +
Booking is simple. We will guide you through the process step by step. To confirm a date in our diary, we ask for a 20% deposit and send you a contract to sign stating the terms of the booking. The remaining balance will be due one week before your event.
HOW FAR DO YOU TRAVEL? +
We’re based in Liverpool, although travel regularly to weddings and events throughout the UK, and even overseas.
CAN I MAKE REQUESTS? +
Absolutely. Soul Sugar are happy and open to discuss your requests. Please get in touch for more information.
WHEN DO I NEED TO BOOK? +
Soul Sugar are a busy band (for obvious reasons!), and so we advise that you book as far in advance as possible.
HOW LONG DO SOUL SUGAR NEED TO SET UP? +
Soul Sugar usually allow around an hour for set-up and soundcheck, depending on details.
